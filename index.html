Dinosaur Time Rescue!

📖 The Story 📖

Little Nate got accidentally sucked into the time of the dinosaurs! Lila, who was feeling like she was alone in the world, saw a mysterious time traveler and bravely jumped into the portal after them to help.

Now, they are trapped in a prehistoric jungle maze. You must help them find and rescue the 5 missing inventors of the time machine. Navigate the maze, swing on vines, dodge the hunting dinosaurs, and forage for meat to distract the speedy Raptors.

If you rescue everyone, prepare to face the terrifying King T-Rex in a final, stealthy tickle-battle on a giant trampoline to escape!
